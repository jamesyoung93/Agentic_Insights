
Digital Transformation and Customer Experience in Service Retail
================================================================

Authors: Anderson, K., White, J., Brown, T.
Published: 2024 - Technology and Service Management

ABSTRACT
--------
Mobile app adoption in retail service settings increases customer satisfaction by 0.6 points (on 5-point scale) and reduces perceived wait times by 23%. App users demonstrate 41% lower churn rates. Integration of loyalty programs with mobile platforms shows synergistic effects.

KEY FINDINGS
-----------
1. Mobile apps increase satisfaction by 0.6 points (12%)
2. App users have 41% lower churn rates
3. Perceived wait time reduced by 23% with mobile ordering
4. Loyalty + mobile app combination yields 67% higher retention

METHODS
-------
Quasi-experimental design, difference-in-differences analysis

KEYWORDS
--------
mobile apps, customer experience, digital transformation, churn
