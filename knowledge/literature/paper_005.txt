
Geographic Segmentation and Market Heterogeneity in Chain Retail
================================================================

Authors: Liu, X., Patel, R., O'Brien, M.
Published: 2023 - Journal of Business Geography

ABSTRACT
--------
Analysis of regional differences in consumer behavior across 45 US markets. Northeast markets show 19% higher price tolerance but 15% lower visit frequency. West Coast customers prioritize quality (mean rating: 4.3/5) over price. Midwest demonstrates strongest loyalty program engagement (72% enrollment).

KEY FINDINGS
-----------
1. Northeast: 19% higher price tolerance, 15% lower frequency
2. West Coast: highest quality preference (4.3/5 avg)
3. Midwest: strongest loyalty enrollment (72%)
4. South: most price-sensitive, highest competitor switching

METHODS
-------
Multi-level modeling, geographic information systems, cluster analysis

KEYWORDS
--------
geographic segmentation, regional differences, market heterogeneity
