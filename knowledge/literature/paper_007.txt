
Promotional Strategy Effectiveness in Repeat-Purchase Service Industries
========================================================================

Authors: Kumar, V., Lee, S., Johnson, M.
Published: 2023 - Journal of Marketing Research

ABSTRACT
--------
Meta-analysis of 47 promotional campaigns finds targeted promotions increase visit frequency by 28% but may reduce transaction value by 12%. Untargeted mass promotions show minimal impact (3% lift). Personalized offers based on purchase history drive 52% higher redemption rates.

KEY FINDINGS
-----------
1. Targeted promotions: +28% visit frequency, -12% ticket size
2. Mass promotions: only 3% lift, poor ROI
3. Personalized offers: 52% higher redemption
4. Promotion fatigue sets in after 4 weekly offers

METHODS
-------
Meta-analysis, controlled experiments, propensity score matching

KEYWORDS
--------
promotions, marketing effectiveness, personalization, ROI
