
Seasonal Demand Patterns and Revenue Optimization in Coffee Retail
==================================================================

Authors: Chen, L., Rodriguez, M., Park, S.
Published: 2024 - International Journal of Hospitality Management

ABSTRACT
--------
We analyze seasonal demand patterns across 250 coffee shop locations over 3 years. Winter months (Oct-Feb) show 18-25% higher transaction volumes. Temperature drops of 10°F correlate with 8% increase in hot beverage sales. Regional variations significant (p<0.05).

KEY FINDINGS
-----------
1. 18-25% higher sales volume in winter months
2. Temperature inversely correlated with hot beverage sales (r=-0.73)
3. Weekend traffic patterns differ by 35% from weekdays
4. Regional preferences vary significantly (ANOVA F=12.4, p<0.001)

METHODS
-------
Time series decomposition, ARIMA modeling, geographic analysis

KEYWORDS
--------
seasonality, demand forecasting, weather impact, revenue
