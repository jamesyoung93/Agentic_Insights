
Competitive Benchmarking and Market Share Dynamics in Urban Coffee Retail
=========================================================================

Authors: Zhang, W., Cohen, R., Murphy, K.
Published: 2024 - Strategic Management Review

ABSTRACT
--------
Examination of competitive dynamics across 8 major US cities. Customers visiting multiple chains monthly ('multi-homers') represent 38% of market but contribute 61% of total category revenue. Quality differentiation sustains price premiums of 15-22% vs. value competitors.

KEY FINDINGS
-----------
1. Multi-homers: 38% of customers, 61% of revenue
2. Quality leaders command 15-22% price premium
3. Store proximity within 0.5 miles reduces customer loyalty 31%
4. Brand switching correlates with new store openings (r=0.68)

METHODS
-------
Market structure analysis, discrete choice modeling, spatial analysis

KEYWORDS
--------
competition, market share, brand switching, positioning
