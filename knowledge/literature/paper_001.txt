
Customer Loyalty Dynamics in Quick-Service Retail: A Time Series Analysis
=========================================================================

Authors: Smith, J., Johnson, K., Williams, R.
Published: 2023 - Journal of Retail Analytics

ABSTRACT
--------
This study examines customer loyalty patterns in quick-service retail environments. Using 18 months of transaction data from 3,200 customers, we find that loyalty program members visit 2.3x more frequently and have 34% higher lifetime value. Mobile app usage correlates strongly with retention (r=0.67, p<0.001).

KEY FINDINGS
-----------
1. Loyalty members visit 2.3x more frequently than non-members
2. Mobile app usage increases retention by 34%
3. Morning customers have 12% higher satisfaction scores
4. Wait times above 7 minutes correlate with 22% drop in return visits

METHODS
-------
Longitudinal analysis, regression modeling, customer segmentation

KEYWORDS
--------
customer loyalty, retention, mobile apps, quick service
